Refund & Cancellation Policy

At Astro Luna, we aim for complete customer satisfaction. However, we have established clear guidelines for refunds and cancellations to ensure a fair experience for both users and astrologers.

1. Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be processed in the following situations:

  • Significant technical failure during a call or chat (e.g., immediate disconnection due to platform error).
  • Multiple deductions for the same transaction.
  • Incomplete services where the astrologer did not respond after session initiation.

2. Cancellation Policy

Instant consultations (Chat/Call) cannot be cancelled once the session has started. For scheduled reports, cancellations are only accepted if the request is made before the astrologer begins processing the chart.

3. Refund Method

Approved refunds are typically credited back to your Astro Luna Wallet within 24-48 hours. These credits can be used for future consultations and do not expire.

4. Request Timeline

Any refund request must be submitted within 7 days of the incident. Please provide the session ID and a briefly describe the issue to our support team.
